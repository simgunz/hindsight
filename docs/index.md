# Hindsight

A hands-free **delayed video mirror** for reviewing your own training form.

The camera films continuously and the screen always shows you from a set number of
seconds ago, looping in real time. Do your exercise, glance at the propped-up phone,
and watch your last rep play back without touching anything — and nothing is ever saved.
